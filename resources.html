Kentucky Digital Newspaper Program Exhibits

KDNP’s exhibit portal makes discovery within some newspaper collections easier than ever. You can browse select collections, such as African American Newspapers, and research highlights like the Hatfields and McCoys. Each newspaper item or collection links to other interesting and useful resources.

UK Libraries Newspapers Resource Guide

The University of Kentucky Libraries provide access to newspapers from around the state, around the nation, and around the world. This resource guide offers tips for finding all newspapers at UK Libraries.

University of Kentucky Libraries Microfilm Holdings Database

Over 60+ years of microfilming at UK Libraries produced nearly 30,000 reels of newspapers on microfilm. You can find the microfilm in this database by Kentucky city or county.

Kentucky Newspapers in Chronicling America

UK Libraries was one of the first six awardee institutions in the National Digital Newspaper Program a partnership between the National Endowment for the Humanities and the Library of Congress. UK Libraries contributed over 500,000 pages of Kentucky’s historic newspapers to Chronicling America.
